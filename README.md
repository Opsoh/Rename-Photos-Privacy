# Rename-Photos-Privacy
Privacy Policy for Rename Photos iOS app

Rename Photos respects your privacy.

Information We Collect
Rename Photos does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Photos Access
The app requests access to your photos only so you can select images to rename and export.

All photo processing happens locally on your device.
Your photos are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Data Storage
Rename Photos does not use servers, accounts, analytics, or tracking.
Temporary files created during export are stored locally and removed automatically by the system.

Third-Party Services
Rename Photos does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Your Control
You can revoke photo access at any time in iOS Settings.

Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact:
support@opsoh.com
